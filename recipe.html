Broccoli Cheese Soup



Ingredients Directions
1 tablespoon butter Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a skillet over medium-high heat.
1/4 cup melted butter Whisk 1/4 cup melted butter and flour together in a large saucepan over medium-low heat; cook until flour loses it's granular texture, adding 1 to 2 tablespoons of milk if necessary to keep the flour from burning, 3 to 4 minutes.
1/4 cup flour Gradually pour milk into flour mixture while whisking constantly. Stir chicken stock into milk mixture. Bring to a simmer; cook until flour taste is gone and mixture is thickened, about 20 minutes. Add broccoli, carrots; simmer until vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.
2 cups milk Stir Cheddar cheese into vegetable mixture until cheese melts. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
2 cups chicken stock ---
1 1/2 cups coarsely chopped broccoli florets ---
1 cup matchstick-cut carrots ---
2 1/2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese ---
salt and ground black pepper to taste ---

Chocolate Chip Cookies



Ingredients Directions
1 cup butter, softened Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
1 cup white sugar Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
1 cup packed brown sugar Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
2 eggs Dissolve baking soda in hot water.
2 teaspoons vanilla extract Add to batter along with salt.
1 teaspoon baking soda Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
2 teaspoons hot water Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Macaroni & Cheese



Ingredients Directions
1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
1/4 cup butter Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat. Once the water is boiling, stir in the macaroni, and return to a boil.
1/4 cup all-purpose flour Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is cooked through but still slightly firm, about 8 minutes. Drain well.
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme Melt 1/4 cup butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. When the butter starts to foam and bubble, stir in the flour; cook on medium heat until flour just begins to turn pale yellow, 3 to 4 minutes.
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper Add thyme, cayenne pepper, and white pepper; cook and stir another minute, then whisk in 1 cup of milk until smooth. Pour in remaining milk and whisk again. Bring the sauce just to a simmer.
1/8 teaspoon white pepper Stir in nutmeg, Worcestershire sauce, and salt; simmer on medium-low heat until thickened, about 8 minutes, whisking often.
3 cups milk Turn heat off, then add 2 1/4 cups of Cheddar cheese; stir until melted and combined. Add Dijon mustard.
1 pinch ground nutmeg Transfer the macaroni into a casserole dish, then pour in the cheese sauce; stir to thoroughly combine sauce with pasta.
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce Mix panko bread crumbs and 1 tablespoon melted butter in a small bowl, and sprinkle crumbs on top of macaroni and cheese.
1 teaspoon salt Sprinkle remaining 3/4 cup of Cheddar cheese on top.
3 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese, divided Bake in the preheated oven until bread crumbs and Cheddar cheese topping are golden brown, about 20 minutes.
3 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese, divided ---
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs ---
1 tablespoon butter, melted ---







Chicken Pot Pie


Ingredients Directions
2 pounds cooked chicken, chopped Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C.) Roll one ball out to fit a 9 inch pie plate.
1 (15 ounce) can mixed vegetables Place bottom crust in pie plate. Roll out top crust and set aside.
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of potato soup In a large bowl, combine chicken, vegetables, potato soup and milk.
1/4 cup milk Season with pepper, thyme, parsley and salt to taste. Pour filling into pie shell.
1/4 teaspoon black pepper Cover with top crust. Seal edges and cut several slits in the top to allow steam to escape.
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until golden brown.
1/4 teaspoon dried parsley ---
salt to taste ---
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie ---
